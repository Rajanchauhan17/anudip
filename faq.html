Orders & Shopping

How do I place an order on SHOPINDIA?

Placing an order is simple:

  • Browse our categories or use the search bar to find products
  • Click on a product to view details and select options
  • Click "Add to Cart" and continue shopping or proceed to checkout
  • Review your cart, apply any discount codes, and click "Checkout"
  • Enter your shipping and payment information
  • Review and confirm your order

You'll receive an order confirmation email with your order details and tracking information.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it, provided it hasn't been processed for shipping. To make changes:

  • Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Find your recent order and click "Modify" or "Cancel"
  • For urgent changes, call our customer service at +91-800-SHOPINDIA

Once an order is shipped, it cannot be modified, but you can return items as per our return policy.

Do you guarantee the authenticity of all products?

Yes! SHOPINDIA guarantees 100% authenticity of all products. We work directly with verified artisans, manufacturers, and authorized dealers. Every product comes with:

  • Certificate of authenticity (where applicable)
  • Detailed product information and origin details
  • Quality assurance guarantee
  • Full refund if authenticity is questioned

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping charges and delivery times?

We offer flexible shipping options across India:

  • Free Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (orders above ₹999)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (₹99 extra)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore (₹199 extra)
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days (charges vary by destination)

Remote areas may require 1-2 additional days. Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your location.

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order is easy:

  • Check your email for the shipping confirmation with tracking number
  • Log into your SHOPINDIA account and visit "My Orders"
  • Use our order tracking page with your order number
  • SMS updates will be sent to your registered mobile number

You can also call our customer service for real-time updates on your order status.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide! International shipping includes:

  • Secure packaging for international transit
  • Customs declaration and documentation
  • Insurance coverage for high-value items
  • Tracking available throughout the journey

Note: International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees imposed by their country.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

SHOPINDIA offers a hassle-free 15-day return policy:

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Tags and labels must be intact
  • Return request must be initiated within 15 days of delivery
  • Some items like food products and personalized items are non-returnable

To initiate a return, log into your account, go to "My Orders," and click "Return Item" next to the product you wish to return.

How long does it take to process refunds?

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 2-3 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 7-10 business days

You'll receive email confirmation once the refund is processed. The actual credit to your account depends on your bank's processing time.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes! We offer exchanges for:

  • Different sizes (clothing, jewelry)
  • Different colors (where available)
  • Damaged or defective items

Exchange requests can be made through your account or by contacting customer service. If the new item costs more, you'll pay the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay
  • Debit Cards: All major Indian bank debit cards
  • Digital Payments: UPI, Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay
  • Net Banking: All major Indian banks
  • Cash on Delivery: Available for orders under ₹10,000
  • EMI Options: 3, 6, 9, 12 months (on select cards)

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! Your payment security is our top priority:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • We never store your complete card details
  • Two-factor authentication for added security
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems

All payments are processed through secure payment gateways like Razorpay and Stripe.

Do you offer any discounts or promotional codes?

Yes! We regularly offer various discounts and promotions:

  • First-time buyers: 10% off with code WELCOME10
  • Festival sales: Special discounts during Indian festivals
  • Bulk orders: Volume discounts for large purchases
  • Newsletter subscribers: Exclusive early access to sales
  • Loyalty program: Earn points with every purchase

Follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest offers!

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  • Click on the "Account" icon in the top right corner
  • Select "Sign Up" and enter your details
  • Verify your email address through the confirmation link
  • Complete your profile with shipping addresses

You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  • Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Check your email for password reset instructions
  • Click the reset link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact customer support.

How do I update my profile and address information?

To update your information:

  • Log into your account and go to "My Profile"
  • Edit your personal information, phone number, or email
  • Go to "Address Book" to add, edit, or delete addresses
  • Save your changes and verify with OTP if required

Keep your information updated to ensure smooth order processing and delivery.
